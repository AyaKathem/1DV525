Linneuniversitet

Linnaeus University (LNU) (Swedish: Linneuniversitetet) is a state university in the Smaland region of Sweden. It has two campuses, one in Vaxjo and one in Kalmar. Linnaeus University was established in 2010 by a merger of former Vaxjo University and Kalmar University , and has been named in honour of botanist Carl Linnaeus
